Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary depending on complexity and scope. A typical website takes 4-8 weeks, while more complex applications may take 3-6 months. We'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

Do you provide ongoing support after launch?

Yes! We offer comprehensive maintenance and support packages including security updates, content updates, performance optimization, and technical support to ensure your project continues to perform optimally.

What's included in your web development service?

Our web development service includes custom design, responsive development, content management system, SEO optimization, performance optimization, security implementation, and post-launch support.

Can you work with our existing brand guidelines?

Absolutely! We can work within your existing brand guidelines while enhancing and extending them for digital applications. If you don't have guidelines, we can help create comprehensive brand standards.

Do you offer digital marketing services?

Yes, we provide comprehensive digital marketing services including SEO, social media marketing, content strategy, PPC advertising, and analytics tracking to help grow your online presence.

What's your payment structure?

We typically work with a 50% deposit to start the project and 50% upon completion. For larger projects, we can arrange milestone-based payments. All payment terms are outlined in our project agreement.
